About

This website is a class project created by Zachary Carter, avid skiier and Alta enthusiast.

He and his family love skiing at Alta often during the winter, and hiking there during the summer.

Zach's favorite runs include Vail Ridge, Cabin Hill, Devil's Playground, and Catherine's area.
